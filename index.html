Connecting Global Scrap Markets

Our Portfolio

Premium Recyclable Materials

We source, inspect, and supply fully sorted scrap metals matching international ISRI code specifications.

HMS 1 & 2 Scrap

Heavy Melting Steel Scrap (ISRI 200-206). Sorted, clean plates, structural sections, and high-density industrial scrap.

Steel & Cast Iron Scrap

Machine parts, structural steels, cast iron frames, auto shredded scrap. Processed and sorted to eliminate impurities.

Copper Scrap

Berry/Candy/Birch high-purity copper wires, busbars, structural tubing, and refinery copper materials.

Aluminium Scrap

Aluminium extrusion profiles (ISRI 6063), alloy wheels, sheet scrap, tense, taint, and clean foil recyclables.

Brass & Stainless Steel

Honey/Ebony brass castings, tubes, dynamic copper-zinc alloys, and Grades 304 / 316 Stainless Steel sheets.

Zinc, Lead & Nickel Scrap

Rains Lead plates, zinc die-cast parts, sheets, and high-value industrial nickel alloy scraps.

+ -

Lead Acid Battery Scrap

Drained lead-acid battery cells (ISRI code Rains), car battery scraps, and heavy industrial cells.

Mixed Metal Residues

Mixed metals containing recyclables, sorted production scrap, and residual metallic raw elements.

How It Works

Ananta Global ensures a structured, secure process for international commodity deals.

1

Submit Inquiry

Provide specifications, target quantity, packing requirements, and target port.

2

Offer & Contract drafting

We supply a formal Proforma Invoice with weight analysis, pricing quotes (FOB/CNF), and terms.

3

Inspection & Container Loading

Consignment loading supervised by third-party inspectors (SGS, CCIC) to certify specifications.

4

Logistics & Document Release

Vessel boarding, Customs clearance, and immediate dispatch of Bills of Lading, Weight certificates, and invoices.
